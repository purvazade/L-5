About the Run Machine/King Kohli

Virat Kohli was raised in Uttam Nagar, Delhi and studied in Vishal Bharti Public School there. In 1998, The West Delhi Cricket Academy was created and Kohli was a part of it. He was just 9-year old boy that time, he was coached by Rajkumar Sharma. His coach told in a interview – “Virat used to practice in a group of small kids of his age, but he always wanted to practice with a group of seniors practicing on the other side there”. “One day, Virat asked his coach to let him practice with the seniors and his coach finally permitted to practice with them. Kohli batted very well n the nets there, but he was hit on his chest. Then too he want to continue with the same group”, his coach added.
